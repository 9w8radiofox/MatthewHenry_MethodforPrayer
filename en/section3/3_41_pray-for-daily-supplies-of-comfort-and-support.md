# 3.40 | Pray for Daily Supplies of Comfort and Support

## We must pray to be supplied with the comforts and supports we daily stand in need of.

## ESV Corporate

O that the favor of the LORD our God may be upon us; prosper the work of our hands upon us; yes, establish the work of our hands. Psalm 90:17(ESV) Save us, we pray, O LORD! O Lord, we pray, give us success! Psalm 118:25(ESV)

May our sons in their youth be as plants full grown, and our daughters like corner pillars cut for the structure of a palace. May our granaries be full, providing all kinds of produce; and may there be no breaking in or going out, no cry of distress in our streets. Blessed are the people to whom such blessings fall! Blessed are the people whose God is the LORD! Psalm 144:12-15(ESV)

Let us be blessed in the city and blessed in the field; Deuteronomy 28:3(ESV) let our basket and our kneading bowl be blessed; let us be blessed when we come in and when we go out. Deuteronomy 28:5-6(ESV)

Let your good providence so order all events concerning us that they may be made to work for good for us, as you have promised they shall for all who love you and are called according to your purpose. Romans 8:28(ESV)

Give us to trust in the LORD and do good, and then we shall dwell in the land and befriend faithfulness; Psalm 37:3(ESV) and be pleased to bring forth our righteousness as the light, and our justice as the noonday. Psalm 37:6(ESV)

Let us be hidden from the lash of the tongue, and not fear destruction when it comes; Job 5:21(ESV) let us be in league with the stones of the field, and let the beasts of the field be at peace with us; let us know that our tent is at peace, and let us inspect our fold and miss nothing. Job 5:23-24(ESV)

And if God will be with us and will keep us in the way that we go during our pilgrimage in this world, and will give us bread to eat and clothing to wear, so that we may come to our heavenly Father’s house in peace, then the LORD shall be our God. Genesis 28:20-21(ESV)

## NIV Corporate

Let our barns be filled with every kind of provision; and let there be no breaching of walls, no going into captivity, no cry of distress in our streets.
