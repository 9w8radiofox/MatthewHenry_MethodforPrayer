# 3.30 | Pray for Grace to Assist you that you may be Courageous in your Duty

## That we may be resolute and courageous in our duty, as those who know that though we may be losers for Christ, we shall not be losers by him in the end.

## ESV Corporate

Lord, teach us to share in suffering as good soldiers of Christ Jesus, 2 Timothy 2:3(ESV) that we may not fear the reproach of men or their revilings, Isaiah 51:7(ESV) nor be ashamed of Christ or of his words, Mark 8:38(ESV) knowing whom we have believed, even one who is able to guard until that Day what we have entrusted to him. 2 Timothy 1:12(ESV)

Though imprisonment and afflictions should await us, Lord, grant that none of these things may move us, and that we may not account life itself as precious to us, if only we may finish our course. Acts 20:23-24(ESV)

Enable us in every way to commend ourselves to God; 2 Corinthians 6:4(ESV) and then to pass through honor and dishonor, through slander and praise, clad with the weapons of righteousness for the right hand and for the left, 2 Corinthians 6:7-8(ESV) as those who account it a very small thing to be judged by any human court, for it is the Lord who judges us. 1 Corinthians 4:3-4(ESV)
