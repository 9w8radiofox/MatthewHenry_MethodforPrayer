# 4.7 | Thank God for the Supports and Comforts of this Life

## For the supports and comforts of this life, which have thus far made the land of my pilgrimage easy and pleasant to me.

## ESV Corporate

Blessed be the Lord, who daily bears us up; God is our salvation. Psalm 68:19(ESV)

You make us lie down in green pastures; you feed us beside the still waters. Psalm 23:2(ESV) You prepare a table before us in the presence of our enemies; you anoint our head, and our cup overflows. Psalm 23:5(ESV)

It may be we were sent out with no moneybag or knapsack, but did we lack anything? Nothing, Lord. Luke 22:35(ESV)

The lamp of God has shone upon our head, and by his light we have walked through darkness, and the friendship of God has been upon our tent. Job 29:3-4(ESV)

You have richly provided us with everything to enjoy, 1 Timothy 6:17(ESV) and have brought plentifully into our hands. Job 12:6(KJV)

Many a time we have eaten and been filled, and have delighted ourselves in your great goodness. Nehemiah 9:25(ESV)

When we remember all the ways that the LORD our God has led us for so many years in this wilderness, Deuteronomy 8:2(ESV) we must here set up a stone and call it Ebenezer, for till now the LORD has helped us. 1 Samuel 7:12(ESV)
