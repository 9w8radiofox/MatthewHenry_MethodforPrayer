# 8.16 | For those under Conviction of Sin

## Those that are under convictions of sin, and begin to be concerned about their souls and their salvation, and to inquire after Christ.

## KJV Corporate

Those that are asking the way to Zion, with their faces thitherward, Jeremiah 50:5(KJV) that are lamenting after the Lord, 1 Samuel 7:2(KJV) and are pricked to the heart for sin, Acts 2:37(KJV) O show them the good and the right way, 1 Samuel 12:23(KJV) and lead them in it.

To those that are asking what they shall do to inherit eternal life, Matthew 19:16(KJV) discover Christ as the way, the truth, and the life, the only true and living way. John 14:6(KJV)

O do not quench the smoking flax, nor break the bruised reed, but bring forth judgment unto victory. Matthew 12:20(KJV) Let the great Shepherd of the sheep gather the lambs in his arms, and carry them in his bosom, and gently lead them, Isaiah 40:11(KJV) and help them against their unbelief. Mark 9:24(KJV)

Let not the red dragon devour the man-child as soon as it is born, but let it be caught up to God and to his throne. Revelation 12:4-5(KJV)
