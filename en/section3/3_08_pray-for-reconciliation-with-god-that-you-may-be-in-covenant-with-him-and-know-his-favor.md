# 3.7 | Pray for Reconciliation with God that you may be in Covenant with Him and Know His Favor

## That I may be taken into covenant with God and admitted into a relationship with him.

## ESV Corporate

Be to us a God, and take us to be your people; Hebrews 8:10(ESV) and make us offer ourselves freely on the day of your power, Psalm 110:3(ESV) though we are no longer worthy to be called your sons. Luke 15:19(ESV) For how would you set us, who have been rebellious, among the sons, and give us the pleasant land? But you have said that we shall call you, Our Father, and not turn from following you. Jeremiah 3:19(ESV) Shall we not therefore from this time call to you, “Our Father, you are the friend of our youth”? Jeremiah 3:4(ESV)

Lord, we hold fast your covenant; Isaiah 56:4(ESV) to you we join ourselves in an everlasting covenant; Jeremiah 50:5(ESV) O that you would make us pass under the rod and bring us into the bond of the covenant, Ezekiel 20:37(ESV) that we may become yours. Ezekiel 16:8(ESV)

Make with us an everlasting covenant, your steadfast, sure love for David. Isaiah 55:3(ESV)

### That I may have the favor of God and an interest in his special love.

## ESV Corporate

We entreat your favor, O God, with all our heart; be gracious to us according to your promise, Psalm 119:58(ESV) for in your favor is life; Psalm 30:5(ESV) yes, your steadfast love is better than life itself. Psalm 63:3(ESV)

LORD, make your face to shine upon us and be gracious to us; LORD, lift up the light of your countenance upon us and give us peace. Numbers 6:25-26(ESV)

Remember us, O LORD, with the favor you show to your people. O help us with your salvation, that we may look upon the prosperity of your chosen ones and may rejoice in the gladness of your nation and may glory with your inheritance. Psalm 106:4-5(ESV)
