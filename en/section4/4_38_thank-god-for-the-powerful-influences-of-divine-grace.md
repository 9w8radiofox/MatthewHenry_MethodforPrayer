# 4.37 | Thank God for the Powerful Influences of Divine Grace

## For the powerful influences of divine grace to sanctify and preserve me, to prevent my falling into sin, and to strengthen me in doing my duty.

## ESV Corporate

You have not quenched the smoldering wick, nor broken the bruised reed, Matthew 12:20(ESV) nor despised the day of small things; Zechariah 4:10(ESV) but having had the help of God, we continue up to this time. Acts 26:22(ESV)

On the day we called, you answered us; and our strength of soul you increased. Psalm 138:3(ESV)

We have been continually with you, you have held our right hand, Psalm 73:23(ESV) when our feet had almost stumbled and our steps had nearly slipped. Psalm 73:2(ESV)

We have reason never to forget your precepts, for by them you have given us life; and if your law had not been our delight, we would have perished in our affliction many a time; Psalm 119:92-93(ESV) for your statutes have been our songs in the house of our sojourning. Psalm 119:54(ESV)

If the Lord had not been our help, our souls would soon have lived in the land of silence: But when we thought, “Our foot slips,” your steadfast love, O LORD, held us up; and when the cares of our hearts are many, your consolations cheer our souls. Psalm 94:17-19(ESV)
