# 8.21 | For Women Near the Time of Child Birth

## When we pray with or for those women that are near the time of travail or in travail.

## KJV Corporate

Lord, thou hast passed this sentence upon the woman that was first in the transgression, 1 Timothy 2:14(KJV) that in sorrow she shall bring forth children. Genesis 3:16(KJV) But let this handmaid of thine be saved in child-bearing, and continue in faith and charity and holiness, with sobriety. 1 Timothy 2:15(KJV) Enable her to cast her burden upon the Lord, and let the Lord sustain her; Psalm 55:22(KJV) and what time she is afraid, grant that she may trust in thee Psalm 56:3(KJV) and may encourage herself in the Lord her God. 1 Samuel 30:6(KJV)

O let not the root be dried up from beneath, nor let the branch be withered or cut off; Job 18:16(KJV) but let both live before thee. Be thou her strong habitation, her rock, and her fortress, give commandment to save her. Psalm 71:3(KJV) And when travail comes upon her, which she cannot escape, be pleased, O Lord, to deliver her; 1 Thessalonians 5:3(KJV) O Lord, make haste to help her; Psalm 40:13(KJV) be thou thyself her help and deliverer, make no tarrying, O our God. Psalm 40:17(KJV) Let her be safely delivered and remember the anguish no more, for joy that a child is born into the world, is born unto thee. John 16:21(KJV)
