# 7.2 | Hallowed be Your Name

## Hallowed be your name.

## ESV Corporate

And now, what is our wish? What is our request? Esther 5:6(ESV) What do we want you to do for us? Matthew 20:32(ESV) This is our heart’s desire and prayer in the first place: Romans 10:1(ESV) Father in heaven, let your name be sanctified. We pray that you may be glorified as a holy God. Leviticus 10:3(ESV)

We desire to exalt the LORD our God, to worship at his footstool, Psalm 99:5(ESV) at his holy mountain; to praise his great and awesome name, Psalm 99:3(ESV) for it is holy, for the LORD our God is holy. Psalm 99:9(ESV) You are holy, enthroned on the praises of Israel. Psalm 22:3(ESV)

We glory in your holy name (and therefore shall our hearts rejoice), Psalm 105:3(ESV) because we have trusted in your holy name, Psalm 33:21(ESV) to which we will always give thanks, and glory in your praise. Psalm 106:47(ESV)

Lord, enable us to glorify your holy name forever, by giving thanks to you with our whole heart, Psalm 86:12(ESV) and by bearing much fruit, for by this is our heavenly Father glorified. John 15:8(ESV) O that we might be for God a name, a praise, and a glory, Jeremiah 13:11(ESV) that being called out of darkness into his marvelous light to be to him a people for his own possession, we may proclaim the excellencies of him who has called us. 1 Peter 2:9(ESV)

O that we may be your children, the work of your hands, that we may sanctify your name and sanctify the Holy One of Jacob and stand in awe of the God of Israel; Isaiah 29:23(ESV) and that we may be to the praise of his glory. Ephesians 1:12(ESV)

Enable us as we have received gifts, so to use them to serve one another, as good stewards of God’s varied grace – in order that in everything God may be glorified through Jesus Christ. 1 Peter 4:10-11(ESV) And if we suffer, enable us to suffer as Christians and to glorify God in that name; 1 Peter 4:16(ESV) for this is our eager expectation and hope: that always Jesus Christ may be honored in our bodies, in life and death. Philippians 1:20(ESV)
