### I must pray to God to work in me charity and brotherly love.

## ESV Corporate

Lord, put upon us that love which binds everything together in perfect harmony, Colossians 3:14(ESV) that we may maintain the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace, Ephesians 4:3(ESV) and may live in love of peace, that the God of love and peace may be with us. 2 Corinthians 13:11(ESV)

Lord, give us to love our neighbor as ourselves with that love which is the fulfilling of the law; Romans 13:9-10(ESV) to love one another earnestly from a pure heart, 1 Peter 1:22(ESV) that by this all men may know that we are Christ’s disciples. John 13:35(ESV)

And as we are taught by God to love one another, give us to abound therein more and more; 1 Thessalonians 4:9-10(ESV) and as we have opportunity, let us do good to everyone; Galatians 6:10(ESV) and so far as it depends on us, let us live peaceably with all men, Romans 12:18(ESV) always pursuing what makes for peace and for mutual upbuilding. Romans 14:19(ESV)

Lord, make us able to love our enemies, to bless those who curse us, to pray for those who despitefully use us, and to do good to those who hate us; Matthew 5:44(KJV) bearing with one another and forgiving one another in love, as Christ has forgiven us. Colossians 3:13(ESV)
