# 4.34 | Thank God for the Hope of Eternal Life and for the Work of the Spirit within you

## For the prospect and hope of eternal life, when time shall be no more.

### Secondly, we must give God thanks for the spiritual mercies bestowed upon us in particular, especially if we are called with an effectual call and have a good work of grace begun in us.

We must bless God for the striving of his Spirit with us, and the admonitions and checks of our own consciences.

## ESV Corporate

We bless you that you have not given us up to a debased mind; Romans 1:28(ESV) that our consciences are not seared; 1 Timothy 4:2(ESV) that you have not said concerning us, “They are joined to idols, leave them alone”; Hosea 4:17(ESV) but that your Spirit is yet contending with us. Genesis 6:3(ESV)

We thank you for the work of the law written on our hearts, our own consciences also bearing witness, and our own conflicting thoughts accusing or excusing us. Romans 2:15(ESV)
