# 7.4 | Your Kingdom Come

## Your Kingdom Come.

## ESV Corporate

In order to the sanctifying and glorifying of your holy name, Father in heaven, let your kingdom come; for yours is the kingdom, O LORD, and you are exalted as head above all. Both riches and honor come from you, and you rule over all. In your hand are power and might, and in your hand it is to make great and to give strength to all. 1 Chronicles 29:11-12(ESV) And we desire to speak of the glory of your kingdom, Psalm 145:11(ESV) for it is an everlasting kingdom, and your dominion endures throughout all generations. Psalm 145:13(ESV)

You rule by your might forever; your eyes keep watch on the nations. O let not the rebellious exalt themselves, Psalm 66:7(ESV) but through the greatness of your power let your enemies come cringing to you. Psalm 66:3(ESV)

O make it to appear that kingship belongs to you, and that you rule over the nations; Psalm 22:28(ESV) make it so evident that they may say among the nations, “The Lord reigns”; Psalm 96:10(ESV) that all mankind may fear and may tell what God has brought about, Psalm 64:9(ESV) and may say, “Surely there is a God who judges on earth.” Psalm 58:11(ESV) Make all the kings of the earth to know that Heaven rules, even that the Most High rules the kingdom of men and gives it to whom he will, Daniel 4:25-26(ESV) and to praise and extol and honor the King of heaven; for all your works are right and your ways are just, and those who walk in pride you are able to humble. Daniel 4:37(ESV)

O let the kingdom of your grace come more and more in the world, that kingdom of God which comes not with signs to be observed, that kingdom of God which is in the midst of men. Luke 17:20-21(ESV) Let it be like leaven in the world, diffusing its relish, till it is all leavened, and like a grain of mustard seed, which, though it is the smallest of all seeds, yet when it has grown, is the greatest among all the garden plants. Matthew 13:31-33(ESV)

Let the kingdom of the world become the kingdom of the Lord and of his Christ. Revelation 11:15(ESV) Take your great power and reign, though the nations rage. Revelation 11:17-18(ESV) Set up your throne where Satan’s throne is, Revelation 2:13(ESV) let every thought be taken captive to obey you, 2 Corinthians 10:5(ESV) and let the law of your kingdom be magnified and made glorious. Isaiah 42:21(ESV)

Let that kingdom of God, which does not consist in talk but in power, 1 Corinthians 4:20(ESV) be set up in all the churches of Christ. Send forth from Zion your mighty scepter and rule by the beauty of holiness. Psalm 110:2-3(ESV)
