# 8.12 | For a Wedding

## Upon occasion of marriage.

## KJV Corporate

Give to those that marry, to marry in the Lord; 1 Corinthians 7:39(KJV) and let the Lord Jesus by his grace come to the marriage and turn the water into wine. John 2:1-10(KJV)

Make them helps meet for each other Genesis 2:18(KJV) and instrumental to promote one another’s salvation; 1 Corinthians 7:16(KJV) and give them to live in holy love, that they may dwell in God, and God in them. 1 John 4:16(KJV)

Let the wife be as a fruitful vine by the side of the house, Psalm 128:3(KJV) and the husband dwell with the wife as a man of knowledge, and let them dwell together as joint heirs of the grace of life, that their prayers be not hindered, 1 Peter 3:7(KJV) and make us all meet for that world where they neither marry nor are given in marriage. Luke 20:35(KJV)
