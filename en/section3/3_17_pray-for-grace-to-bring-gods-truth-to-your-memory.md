# 3.16 | Pray for Grace to Bring God’s Truth to your Memory

## To help our memory, that the truths of God may be ready to us whenever we have occasion to use them.

## ESV Corporate

Lord, let your Spirit teach us all things and bring to our remembrance all that you have said to us, John 14:26(ESV) that the word of Christ may dwell richly in us in all wisdom and spiritual understanding. Colossians 3:16(ESV)

Lord, grant that we may pay much closer attention to what we have heard, lest we drift away from it; Hebrews 2:1(ESV) and may we hold fast to the word that has been preached to us, and not believe in vain. 1 Corinthians 15:2(ESV)

Lord, make us ready and competent in the Scriptures, Acts 18:24(ESV) that we may be competent, equipped for every good work; 2 Timothy 3:17(ESV) and, being well trained for the kingdom of heaven, may we, like a good master of a house, bring out of our treasure what is new and what is old. Matthew 13:52(ESV)
