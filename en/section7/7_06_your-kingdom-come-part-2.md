# 7.5 | Your Kingdom Come (Part 2)

## Your Kingdom Come (continued)

## ESV Corporate

Where the strong man, fully armed, has long guarded his palace, and his goods are safe, let Christ, who is stronger than he, attack him and take away his armor in which he trusted and divide the spoil. Luke 11:21-22(ESV)

O give to the Son of man dominion and glory and a kingdom, that all peoples, nations, and languages may serve him, Daniel 7:14(ESV) and the judgment may be given for the saints of the Most High. Daniel 7:22(ESV)

Let the kingdom of your grace come more and more in our land and the places where we live. There let the word of God speed ahead and be honored, 2 Thessalonians 3:1(ESV) and let not the kingdom of God be taken away from us, which is what we deserve, and given to a people producing its fruits. Matthew 21:43(ESV)

Let the kingdom of your grace come into our hearts, that they may be temples of the Holy Spirit. 1 Corinthians 6:19(ESV) Let no iniquity get dominion over us. Psalm 119:133(ESV) Ruin, ruin, ruin the power of corruption there, and let him come who has right to our hearts, and give them to him; Ezekiel 21:27(ESV) make us offer ourselves freely, more and more freely, on the day of your power. Psalm 110:3(ESV) Rule in us by the power of truth, that, being of the truth, we may always hear Christ’s voice, John 18:37(ESV) and may not only call him ‘Lord, Lord,’ but do what he tells us. Luke 6:46(ESV) And let the love of Christ command us and control us, 2 Corinthians 5:14(ESV) and the fear of him be before our eyes, that we may not sin. Exodus 20:20(ESV)

O let the kingdom of your glory be hastened; we believe it will come. We look for the Savior, the Lord Jesus, Philippians 3:20(ESV) to come on the clouds of heaven with power and great glory. Matthew 24:30(ESV) We hope that he shall appear to our joy; Isaiah 66:5(KJV) we love his appearing; we are waiting for and hastening the coming of the day of God; 2 Peter 3:12(ESV) make us ready for it, Matthew 24:44(ESV) that we may then raise our heads with joy, knowing that our redemption is drawing near. Luke 21:28(ESV) And, O that we may have such firstfruits of the Spirit that we ourselves may groan inwardly as we wait eagerly for adoption as sons, the redemption of our bodies; Romans 8:23(ESV) and may we have a desire to depart and be with Christ, which is best of all. Philippians 1:23(ESV)

Blessed Jesus, be with your ministers and people as you have said, “Always, to the end of the age.” Matthew 28:20(ESV) And then, as you have said, “Surely I am coming soon.” Come, Lord Jesus! Come quickly! Revelation 22:20(ESV) When the mystery of God shall be fulfilled, Revelation 10:7(ESV) make haste, our beloved, and be like a gazelle or a young stag on the mountains of spices. Song of Solomon 8:14(ESV)

## NIV Corporate

Where the strong man, fully armed, has long guarded his own house, and his possessions are safe, let Christ, who is stronger, attack him and take away the armor in which he trusted and divide up the spoils. Luke 11:21-22(NIV)

O give to the Son of man authority, glory, and sovereign power, that all peoples, nations, and men of every language may worship him, Daniel 7:14(NIV) and judgment may be pronounced in favor of the saints of the Most High. Daniel 7:22(NIV)

Let the kingdom of your grace come more and more in our land and the places where we live. There let the word of God spread rapidly and be honored, 2 Thessalonians 3:1(NIV) and do not let the kingdom of God be taken away from us, which is what we deserve, and given to a people who will produce its fruit. Matthew 21:43(NIV)

Let the kingdom of your grace come into our hearts, that they may be the temples of the Holy Spirit. 1 Corinthians 6:19(NIV) Let no sin rule over us. Psalm 119:133(NIV) A ruin! A ruin! Make the power of corruption there a ruin! And let him come to whom our hearts rightfully belong, and give them to him; Ezekiel 21:27(NIV) make us willing, more and more willing, on your day of battle. Psalm 110:3(NIV) Rule in us by the power of truth, that being on the side of truth, we may always listen to Christ’s voice, John 18:37(NIV) and may not only call him, ‘Lord, Lord,’ but do what he says. Luke 6:46(NIV) And let Christ’s love command us and compel us, 2 Corinthians 5:14(NIV) and his fear be with us to keep us from sinning. Exodus 20:20(NIV)

O let the kingdom of your glory be hastened; we believe it will come. We eagerly await the Savior, the Lord Jesus, Philippians 3:20(NIV) to come on the clouds of the sky, with power and great glory. Matthew 24:30(NIV) We hope that he shall appear to our joy; Isaiah 66:5(KJV) we long for his appearing; 2 Timothy 4:8(NIV) we are looking forward to the day of God and speed its coming; 2 Peter 3:12(NIV) make us ready for it, Matthew 24:44(NIV) that we may then lift up our heads with joy, knowing that our redemption draws near. Luke 21:28(NIV) And, O that we may have such firstfruits of the Spirit that we ourselves may groan inwardly as we wait eagerly for our adoption as sons, the redemption of our bodies; Romans 8:23(NIV) and may we have a desire to depart and be with Christ, which is best of all. Philippians 1:23(NIV)

Blessed Jesus, be with your ministers and people, as you have said, ‘Always, to the very end of the age.’ Matthew 28:20(NIV) And then, as you have said, ‘Yes, I am coming soon,’ even so, Come, Lord Jesus. Come quickly! Revelation 22:20(NIV) When the mystery of God shall be accomplished, Revelation 10:7(NIV) come away, our lover, and be like a gazelle or like a young stag on the spice-laden mountains. Song of Solomon 8:14(NIV)
