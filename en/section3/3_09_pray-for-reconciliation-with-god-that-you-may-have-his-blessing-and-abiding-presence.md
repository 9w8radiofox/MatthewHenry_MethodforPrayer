# 3.8 | Pray for Reconciliation with God that you may Have His Blessing and Abiding Presence

## That we may have the blessing of God.

## ESV Corporate

O God, be gracious to us and bless us and make your face to shine upon us; Psalm 67:1(ESV) yes, let God, our God, give us his blessing. Psalm 67:6(ESV)

May the LORD, he who made heaven and earth, bless us from Zion; Psalm 134:3(ESV) bless us with every spiritual blessing in the heavenly places in Christ Jesus. Ephesians 1:3(ESV)

O that you would bless us indeed! 1 Chronicles 4:10(KJV) Command the blessing upon us, even life forevermore; Psalm 133:3(ESV) for you bless, O LORD, and it shall be blessed. 1 Chronicles 17:27(ESV)

Let us receive blessing from the LORD, even righteousness from the God of our salvation. Psalm 24:5(ESV)

Have you but one blessing? Indeed, you have many blessings! Bless us, even us also, O our Father; Genesis 27:38(ESV) yes, let the blessing of Abraham come to us, which comes to the Gentiles through faith; Galatians 3:14(ESV) and the blessing of Jacob, for we will not let you go unless you bless us. Genesis 32:26(ESV)
