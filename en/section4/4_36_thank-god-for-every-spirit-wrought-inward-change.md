# 4.35 | Thank God for Every Spirit-Wrought Inward Change

## I must bless God if there is a saving change wrought in me by his blessed Spirit.

## ESV Corporate

And has God by his grace transferred us out of the kingdom of darkness into the kingdom of his dear Son? Colossians 1:13(ESV) Has he called us into the fellowship of Jesus Christ, 1 Corinthians 1:9(ESV) and brought us near by his blood, who by nature were far off? Ephesians 2:13(ESV) Not to us, O LORD, not to us, but to your name we give glory. Psalm 115:1(ESV)

We give thanks to God always for those to whom the gospel has come not only in word, but in power and in the Holy Spirit and with full conviction. 1 Thessalonians 1:5(ESV)

You have loved us with an everlasting love; therefore you have continued your faithfulness to us. Jeremiah 31:3(ESV) You have led us with cords of kindness, with the bands of love. Hosea 11:4(ESV)

When the strong man, fully armed, guarded his palace in our hearts, and his goods were safe, it was One stronger than he who attacked him and took away his armor in which he trusted and divided the spoil. Luke 11:21-22(ESV)
