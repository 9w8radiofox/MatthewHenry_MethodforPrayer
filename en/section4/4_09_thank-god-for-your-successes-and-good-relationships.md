# 4.8 | Thank God for your Successes and Good Relationships

## For success in our callings and affairs, comfort in relations, and comfortable places of abode.

## ESV Corporate

It is God who equips us with strength and makes our way blameless, Psalm 18:32(ESV) who has blessed the work of our hands; Job 1:10(ESV) and it may be that though our beginning was small, yet our latter days will be very great. Job 8:7(ESV)

Our houses have been safe from fear, and there has been no rod of God upon us; Job 21:9(ESV) so that glad songs of salvation have been in our tents from day to day. Psalm 118:15(ESV)

With our staff it may be we have crossed this Jordan, and now we have become two camps; Genesis 32:10(ESV) and it is God who settles the solitary in homes. Psalm 68:6(ESV)

If we have enjoyed life with our relations, Ecclesiastes 9:9(ESV) and they have been to us as a lovely deer, a graceful doe, Proverbs 5:19(ESV) we must give you thanks for it; for every creature is that to us, and no more, that you make it to be.
