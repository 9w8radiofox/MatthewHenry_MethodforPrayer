# 7.7 | Your Will be Done (Part 2)

## Your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven.

## ESV Corporate

Lord, give grace to each of us to know and do the will of our Father in heaven. Matthew 12:50(ESV) This is the will of God, our sanctification. 1 Thessalonians 4:3(ESV) Now may the God of peace sanctify us completely. 1 Thessalonians 5:23(ESV) O let us be filled with the knowledge of your will in all spiritual wisdom and understanding, Colossians 1:9(ESV) and equip us with everything good that we may do your will. Hebrews 13:21(ESV) O let the time that is past suffice for doing what the flesh wants to do, 1 Peter 4:3(ESV) and to have walked according to the course of this world. Ephesians 2:2(ESV) And from henceforth, grant that it may always be our food and drink to do the will of our Father and to accomplish his work; John 4:34(ESV) not to do our own will but his who sent us, John 6:38(ESV) that we may be of those who will enter the kingdom of heaven, Matthew 7:21(ESV) and not those who will receive a severe beating. Luke 12:47(ESV)

Lord, give grace to others also to know and do your will; to discern what is the will of God, what is good and acceptable and perfect; Romans 12:2(ESV) not to be foolish, but to understand what the will of the Lord is; Ephesians 5:17(ESV) and then give them to stand mature and fully assured in all the will of God: Colossians 4:12(ESV) And let us all serve our generations according to that will. Acts 13:36(ESV)

And when we have done the will of God, let us receive what is promised; Hebrews 10:36(ESV) and let that part of the will of God be done. Lord, let the word that you have spoken concerning your servants be established forever, and do as you have spoken. 1 Chronicles 17:23(ESV)

We rejoice that your will is done in heaven; that the holy angels do your word and always obey the voice of your word; Psalm 103:20(ESV) that they always see the face of our Father. Matthew 18:10(ESV) And we lament that your will is so little done on earth, so many of the children of men being captured by Satan to do his will. 2 Timothy 2:26(ESV) O that this earth may be made more like heaven! And saints more like the holy angels! And that we who hope to be shortly like angels in heaven, Matthew 22:30(ESV) may now, like them, never cease from praising him; Revelation 4:8(ESV) may now, like them, resist and withstand Satan; Daniel 10:13(ESV) may be as a flaming fire, Psalm 104:4(ESV) in swift flight, Daniel 9:21(ESV) and may go straight forward, wherever the Spirit goes; Ezekiel 1:12(ESV) may serve for the good of others, Hebrews 1:14(ESV) and thus may come into communion with the innumerable angels in festal gathering.
