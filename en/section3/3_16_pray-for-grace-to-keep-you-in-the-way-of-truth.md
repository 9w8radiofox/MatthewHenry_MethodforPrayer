# 3.15 | Pray for Grace to Keep you in the Way of Truth

## To lead me into and keep me in the way of truth; and if in anything I am in error, to rectify my mistake.

## ESV Corporate

Let the Spirit of truth guide us into all truth, John 16:13(ESV) and make us understand how we have gone astray. Job 6:24(ESV)

Teach us what we do not see; Job 34:32(ESV) and enable us to test everything, so as to hold fast to what is good. 1 Thessalonians 5:21(ESV)

Lord, grant that we may not be as children, tossed to and fro and carried about by every wind of doctrine, by human cunning; rather, speaking the truth in love, may we grow up in every way into Christ, who is the head. Ephesians 4:14-15(ESV)

Lord, give us so to do your will, as that we may know that the teaching is from God; John 7:17(ESV) and so to know the truth, as that the truth may set us free, John 8:32(ESV) may set us free indeed. John 8:36(ESV)

Enable us, we pray, to follow the pattern of sound words that we have heard in the faith and love that are in Christ Jesus, 2 Timothy 1:13(ESV) and to continue in what we have learned and have firmly believed. 2 Timothy 3:14(ESV)
